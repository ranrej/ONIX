Instalation Services

We offer a range of electrical services for condos and homes, including the installation of the Tesla Wall Charger and NEMA 14/50 EV charger to provide convenient and efficient charging solutions for electric vehicles. Our expertise also extends to installing new plugs for washers and dryers, along with baseboard heaters, digital timers, dimmer switches, slim pot lights, and basement wiring. Additionally, we can upgrade your electrical system with a new 600 volts panel and transformers, ensuring optimal performance and safety. Count on us for all your electrical needs and more.

Repair and Replacement

We provide emergency 24/7 service for electrical needs, ensuring prompt assistance whenever it's required. Our team specializes in upgrading electrical panels from 60 or 100 amps to a more robust 200 amps, enhancing the power capacity for homes and condos. Additionally, we offer professional fuse-to-breaker upgrades and tackle renovation projects, including the removal of outdated knob-and-tube wiring for enhanced safety and functionality.
