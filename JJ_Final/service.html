Electrical Repair

At Ez Voltage, we offer 24-hour electrical services to ensure your safety and protect your property. Our team handles emergency repairs, troubleshooting, new wiring, upgrades, and general repairs, including circuit breaker issues, lighting repairs, and surge protection. With certified contractors ready to respond immediately, we deliver reliable solutions for all your electrical needs.

EV Charging Station

Upgrade your home with our expert Residential EV Charging Station Installation. Our certified electricians complete the job in one day, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Services include site assessment, permitting, Level 1 and Level 2 installation, seamless integration with your electrical system, and ongoing maintenance. Enjoy home charging convenience, fuel savings, off-peak electricity rates, increased property value, and a reduced carbon footprint.

Panel Upgrades & Knob-Tube Wiring

At Ez Voltage, we provide expert panel upgrades and knob and tube wiring replacements for safe and efficient power distribution. We handle all panel types, from 100-amp for medium homes to 400-amp for larger properties. Certified by the Electrical Safety Authority (ESA), our work meets safety standards, and you'll receive a certificate upon completion, enhancing your home's value. Choose us for our expertise and commitment to safety.

Commercial Services

We provide full electrical services: inspections for safety and code compliance, prompt repairs to reduce downtime, and regular maintenance to extend equipment life. We handle system upgrades, infrared scans for overheating, emergency lighting checks, and load testing to prevent overloads. We also install EV charging stations for commercial properties. Choose us for quick, safety-focused service from certified contractors.
